Most homeowners call their insurance company before they know what's actually wrong with the roof. That's how claims get denied — or settled for half what they're worth. Here's the right order of operations.

1

Get a roof inspection first.

Before you ever pick up the phone to your insurer. We tell you honestly whether you have damage that meets a claim threshold.

2

Document everything, fast.

Exterior shots, ceiling stains, hail marks, fallen branches. The first 24 hours after damage is when documentation is cleanest.

3

Have your contractor meet the adjuster.

Adjusters miss damage. Having an experienced roofer on the ladder with them is the biggest single factor in a fair payout.
